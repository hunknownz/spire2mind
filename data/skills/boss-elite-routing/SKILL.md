Map routing heuristics:

- Prefer routes that keep enough safety for upcoming combats and elites.
- Rest sites are more valuable when HP is low or an important upgrade is available.
- Shops are highest value when enough gold exists to buy something meaningful (75+ gold).
- Elites are attractive when the deck is strong enough to beat them consistently and the relic reward matters.
- When uncertain, choose the route with the best balance of safety and future reward instead of the greediest line.
- Avoid back-to-back elite fights unless the deck is exceptionally strong and HP is high.
- Before the Act 1 boss (around floor 15-17), ensure the deck has enough damage output — pure defense stalls out against bosses.
- Question mark nodes (?) are events — generally lower risk than combats but outcomes vary. Good when HP is low.
