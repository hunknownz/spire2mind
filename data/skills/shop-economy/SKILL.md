Merchant heuristics:

- Check card removal first; it is often one of the strongest purchases, especially early when the deck has many weak starters.
- Strong relics usually beat medium cards when gold is limited.
- Do not buy weak filler cards just because gold is available — every card added dilutes the deck.
- Skip the shop quickly when nothing meaningfully improves the deck or survival.
- Potions are worthwhile when they solve an immediate upcoming problem (elite/boss), not just because they are cheap.
- If gold exceeds 150, something has gone wrong — spend it aggressively on removal, relics, or the strongest card available.
- Compare each purchase against the alternative of keeping gold for a future shop with better options.
